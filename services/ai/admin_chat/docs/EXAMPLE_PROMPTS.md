## üí° Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific**: Instead of "show claims", try "show me claim volume by state"
2. **Use Natural Language**: "How many..." works better than "COUNT..."
3. **Request Charts**: Add "generate a chart" or "show me a graph" for visualizations
4. **Specify Timeframes**: "this month", "last 30 days", "October 2025"
5. **Ask for Comparisons**: "compare X and Y" generates comparison charts

## üìã Common Query Patterns

### Counting
- "How many [entity] are [condition]?"
- "Count of [entity] by [group]"
- "Total number of [entity]"

### Grouping
- "[Entity] by [category]"
- "Breakdown of [entity] by [group]"
- "[Metric] grouped by [field]"

### Filtering
- "[Entity] where [condition]"
- "[Entity] in [location/timeframe]"
- "[Entity] with [attribute]"

### Aggregations
- "Total [metric] by [group]"
- "Average [metric] per [category]"
- "Sum of [metric] by [field]"

## üé® Visualization Requests

To get charts and graphs, include these phrases:
- "generate a chart"
- "show me a graph"
- "create a visualization"
- "display as chart"
- "plot [data]"

- Generate Plotly configuration
- Generate Chart.js configuration  
- Create PNG chart images
