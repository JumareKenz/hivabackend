## What is MCP?

**Model Context Protocol (MCP)** is a standardized way for AI systems to access your business data. Think of it as upgrading from a custom-built interface to a universal standard that works with any AI system.

### Simple Analogy

- **Before (Legacy)**: Each AI needs custom wiring to access your data
- **After (MCP)**: Any AI can plug into a standard outlet
